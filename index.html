What is Find Your Story?

We know the importance of the language artsas a tool for learning, professional skill, and form of expression. We're asocial enterprisethat hopes to reignite Canadian youth engagement in the language arts and improve access to reading and writing education for students. We're hoping to do so through community-based workshops and student resources!


Our mission is to reignite Canadian youth engagement in the language arts, and improve access to language education for students who need support. We plan on accomplishing this through various accessible educational programs and different resources.

1100+ Youth Followers

We share our information through our social media platforms to inform students about our amazing opportunities!

300+ Total Attendees

Through our several programs, we've had several students come to learn more about writing and the English Language Arts.

20+ countries

As a youth organization, we hope to expand our demographic across the world, focusing on literary learning to help them thrive!

Dear Love/Time/Death

Throughout the month of February, 37 talented students participated in Dear Love/Time/Death, Find Your Storys first writing competition. They submitted emotional and heartfelt letters reflecting on the choices of 2020 through the concepts of love, time, and death.

Essay 101 Find Your Story is excited to present ESSAY 101, a series of virtual formal writing workshops about informed research, structuring a paragraph, literary VS comparative essays, and more. Learn more here
Dear Love Time Death Dear Love Time Death is Find Your Storys first writing competition of 2021. The purpose of this competition is to reflect on the choices throughout 2020, the effects of said choices, and the opportunities for growth through the epistolary genre. Learn more here
News For Youth News for Youth is a free, five-week online writing workshop for youth to learn about the world of journalism and become global citizens! Earn $25 for submitting and publishing your work, hear from real journalists, and join a community of passionate writers
